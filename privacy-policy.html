Privacy Policy · Updated: May 27, 2026

Who we are

InveStory is an app for tracking personal financial assets: deposits, currencies, crypto, stocks, loans, precious metals and custom sections.

Data you enter

You may enter amounts, asset names, dates, rates, notes, top-up history, loan details and other financial information. This data is used only to provide app features.

Where data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not create accounts and do not upload your financial data to our servers.

Online features

If you use online exchange rates, crypto or stock prices, the app contacts external services. Requests may include a ticker, currency pair and IP address processed by that external service.

Export and import

Export creates a backup file on your device. If you send it to cloud storage or a messenger, that service handles it under its own rules.

Purchases and subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive your payment card details.

Analytics and advertising

The current version does not use advertising trackers or collect behaviour analytics. This policy will be updated if that changes.

Security

You are responsible for protecting your device and backup files. We recommend using screen lock and storing backups in trusted locations only.

Your choices

You can delete all data in app settings or by uninstalling the app. You can export a backup before deletion.

Important

InveStory is not a financial advisor. Calculations, rates and forecasts are informational and may differ from actual bank or market values.
