Privacy Policy

Updated: June 15, 2026

Overview

Kapital is a personal finance tracking application. It helps users record and monitor their own assets, deposits, currencies, crypto, stocks, metals and liabilities.

Local data storage

Your financial data is stored locally on your device/browser storage. The app does not require an account and does not operate as a bank, broker or payment provider.

Network requests

The app may request online market prices, crypto prices or currency rates when you use online update features. These requests are used only to display current reference values in the app.

Financial disclaimer

Kapital does not provide financial, tax or investment advice. Calculations and market data are informational and may differ from actual bank, broker or exchange values.
